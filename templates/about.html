Decode Your Future

The journey of InternMatch began not in a boardroom, but in late-night study sessions and conversations among friends. As students of Biju Patnaik University of Technology, we navigated the same challenges every student faces: the overwhelming search for meaningful internships, the confusion of identifying the right career path, and the feeling that great opportunities were just out of reach. We knew there had to be a better way. This shared frustration became our mission. We saw a "glitch" in the system—a disconnect between talented students and the professional world. We decided to become the ones to fix it. That's how GLITCH HUNTER was born. Fueled by code and a passion for problem-solving, we channeled our experiences into creating a platform that we wished we had. InternMatch is the result. It’s an intelligent, AI-powered tool designed to eliminate the noise and guide BPUT students directly to opportunities that match their unique skills and aspirations. Our vision is to empower every student to build a future they are excited about, turning ambition into achievement.
